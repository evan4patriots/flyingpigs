Dietary Information

The diet of the flying pig is lighting bugs. This supports their flying abilities. They glow happiness as a result.

Flying pigs also ingest moisture from clouds via passageways in their snouts. This is the pig’s primary source for staying hydrated in flight. Domesticated flying pigs eat high in protein “slop” for maximum mass. Read also: www.air-to-table.org www.lightingbugsupply.com
