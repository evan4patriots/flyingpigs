Animal Behavior

The flying pig uses its very dependable nose to sniff out its favorite food – lighting bugs. The lightning bugs provide it with essential magical nutrients that supplement the pig’s ability to fly.

The flying pig is very good at protecting itself from predators that are alive as they can smell a mile away. However, due to their blindness, their lifespan is short because it runs into things it cannot smell such as planes, helicopters etc.

When Flying pigs get tired, they find a rainbow, follow it to the end and curl up next to its magical pot of gold. This is where they derive the magic necessary to fly.

The behavior of the pig depends on how close it is to a rainbow. The closer a rainbow, the happier the pig. Flying pigs can be found floating about, ready to cuddle in between sunshine and rain showers. However, if you see a flying pig in the absence of a rainbow, it is not recommended you approach. Things can turn deadly.
