Interesting facts about the majestic flying pig

  • Flying pigs are genderless!
  • Flying pigs do not need to migrate South for the winter, but will in fact maintain their body temperature by adjusting flight altitude.
  • Evolution and flights around the world have led to what experts estimate as over a hundred undiscovered species of flying pig!
  • It is legal in Texas to hunt flying pigs via helicopter due to their threat to Texan farmer fields and property.
